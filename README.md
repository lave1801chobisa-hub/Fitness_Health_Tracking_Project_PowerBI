# Fitness_Health_Tracking_Project_PowerBI
Analyzed 350 health records in Power BI: avg steps 10,940, calories 958, sleep 6.6 hrs (only 40% meet targets). Gym yields highest calorie burn; ages 36–50 lead overall. Diet shows minimal effect—workout type and consistency drive results. Yoga and cycling are most efficient. Engagement: 98.9%. Conclusion: consistency + activity type matter most
